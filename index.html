🎮 How to Play & Controls

Default Keyboard Mapping:

  • D-Pad: Arrow Keys
  • A / B Buttons: X and Z
  • L / R Bumpers: Q and W
  • Start / Select: Enter and Shift

Customizing Controls: Once the game boots, look for the small Menu/Gamepad Icon at the bottom or top of the game screen. Clicking this opens the Emulator settings where you can remap your keyboard, connect a Bluetooth controller, or adjust save states!
